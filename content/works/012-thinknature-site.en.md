We designed the corporate website for Think Nature, a nature tech company that
uses biodiversity and natural capital big data to help build a society that
coexists with nature.

To communicate Think Nature's concept of biodiversity in an intuitive way, we
expressed the branching structure of relationships among living things as a
motion key visual.


Even though the subject is complex and highly specialized, we designed the
experience so visitors could grasp it intuitively.

CurioSwitch mainly handled planning and production management for the site, as
well as the key visual motion.
