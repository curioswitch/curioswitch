CurioSwitch LLC ("we," "us," or "our") has established this Privacy Policy
("Policy") to appropriately protect the personal information of users in
connection with the various services we provide (the "Services"). We handle
information relating to users as described below.

## 1. Collection of Personal Information

When providing the Services, we may collect the following information:

- Contact information such as name, email address, and phone number
- Usage history, access date and time, device information, browser information,
  and IP address
- Cookies and access data collected through analytics tools
- Information provided through inquiries and surveys

## 2. Purposes of Use

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- To provide, operate, and improve the Services
- To provide user support and respond to inquiries
- To send notices regarding new features, updates, campaigns, and similar
  matters
- To analyze usage and consider marketing initiatives
- To prevent misconduct and maintain security
- To respond as required by laws, regulations, and terms

## 3. Provision to Third Parties

We do not provide personal information to third parties except in the following
cases:

- When we have obtained the user's consent
- When required by law
- When it is necessary to provide such information to subcontractors within the
  scope required to achieve the intended purpose

## 4. Supervision of Subcontractors

When we entrust the handling of personal information to subcontractors, we will
provide appropriate management and supervision.

## 5. Cookies and Analytics

The Services may use cookies and tools such as Google Analytics to improve
convenience and analyze usage. Information collected through these tools does
not identify individuals.

## 6. Management of Personal Information

We will take reasonable security measures to prevent unauthorized access,
leakage, loss, or similar incidents involving personal information.

## 7. Disclosure, Correction, and Deletion of Personal Information

If a user requests disclosure, correction, deletion, suspension of use, or
similar action regarding their personal information, we will respond promptly in
accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

## 8. Compliance and Review

We comply with laws, regulations, and other standards relating to personal
information, and we will continuously review and improve this Policy.

## 9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Policy, please contact us.
