The other day, we gave a special lecture at Institute of Science Tokyo on the theme of "design thinking."\
During the lecture, we shared practical processes with the students, including the importance of user research, methods for identifying challenges, and how to move quickly through prototyping.

After the lecture, we received many questions, and it became a deeply rewarding session in which we strongly felt the students' curiosity and enthusiasm.
