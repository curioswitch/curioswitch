At "Open House 2025," hosted by NHK Science & Technology Research
Laboratories from May 29 to June 1, 2025, we worked with MESON Inc. on a
concept exhibition exploring future viewing experiences using AR glasses.

This exhibition introduced a vision of how AR glasses could expand the
experience of viewing content into the surrounding space.

- Watching the same content while communicating through live video with a friend
  in another location
- Replaying a scene you want to see again
- Sharing excitement through reaction input and playback of other people's
  reactions

CurioSwitch handled planning, experience design, and exhibition operations
support.
